# 20: Community Health Volunteer (CHV) Field Work

A community health volunteer works within a community providing basic health services.

As part of their work:

* They encounter individuals with symptoms and document relevant details about each case
* They determine and record outcomes such as diagnosis and treatment given
* They keep track of the supplies they have available while performing their duties
* They receive additional supplies from time to time and note those updates
* Their activities happen continuously as part of their day-to-day responsibilities

This work results in:

* Recorded cases
* Documented treatments
* Ongoing awareness of available supplies
* A history of what was done, when, and by whom
