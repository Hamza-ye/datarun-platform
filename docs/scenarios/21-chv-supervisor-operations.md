# 21: Supervisor Visit and Assessment

A supervisor is responsible for periodically visiting community health volunteers to review their work.

During a visit:

* The supervisor meets with a specific volunteer at a point in time
* They review aspects of the volunteer's work using a set of criteria
* They observe, verify, or question the information recorded by the volunteer
* They document their observations, including both structured inputs and open notes
* They may identify gaps, inconsistencies, or areas for improvement

Each visit results in:

* A record of the visit
* Documented observations and assessments
* A point-in-time perspective on the volunteer's work
