# 04: Review and Judgment by Another Person

Work done by one person is reviewed by another before it's considered final. The reviewer may accept it, send it back for correction, or flag concerns.

Until the review happens, the work sits in a waiting state. Delays in review are noticeable. The outcome of the review — whether it was accepted, returned, or questioned — is part of the record.

This results in:

* Work that passes through a judgment step before being finalized
* A visible trail of who reviewed what, when, and what they decided
* Awareness of where things are waiting and for how long
